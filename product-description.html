GitHub Repository Marketplace for Developers

Buy, sell, & share your GitHub repos effortlessly with Shfty. Expand your coding reach & unlock new opportunities!

Wanted to solve a few problems:

  • Helps developers earn money. Now 1 person can create a large project quickly with the help of AI.
  • Help businesses and consumers own their software instead of paying extremely large monthly fees.
  • A central location where people can find other open source projects or other software that they can buy or sell and host themselves.
  • A place to put all my failed startups to let other people build upon.
